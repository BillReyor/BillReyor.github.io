EmeraldCon Underground Hacker Conference

About EmeraldCon

EmeraldCon is an underground hacker conference that runs adjacent to the Hackers on Planet Earth (HOPE) conference. It's a gathering of like-minded individuals who are passionate about cybersecurity, privacy, and the exploration of technology.
